Codehub Marathon

Central Park Area, 9 september 2024

Codehub Marathon 5K: A short, challenging run in the heart of Jakarta

Start

Starting at sea level, this 5K route starts from Jalan Jenderal Sudirman, one of the main roads in downtown Jakarta, with excitement that immediately injects adrenaline into your run.

Challenge

The challenge here is speed and consistency. This 5 kilometer run demands your stamina as you navigate a long straight road, with busy traffic and tall buildings in the background.

Iconic Spots

Along the route, you will pass city landmarks such as Bundaran HI, Gelora Bung Karno, and various skyscrapers of Jakarta, giving you the opportunity to feel the pulse of busy urban life.

Straight Road Challenge

Even without major inclines, the challenge on this route is maintaining a rhythm of speed along the straight road and dealing with changes in road surface, including sidewalks and public transport lanes.

Finish Line

Towards the end, the route will lead you to the finish line in an upbeat atmosphere amidst the hustle and bustle of the city, providing a satisfying sense of accomplishment and victory.

Celebration Awaits

Completing the 5K at the Codehub Marathon is a moment of personal achievement, greeted with the sparkling view of downtown Jakarta in the background.
